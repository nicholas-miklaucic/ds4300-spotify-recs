Spotify Rec Engine

Spotify Rec Engine lets you get new song recommendations for a Spotify playlist. It tries to find songs that are similar to the ones already in your playlist, so it might help you find a new artist.

To get recs for a playlist, simply go to Spotify and find a playlist. The playlist URL will look something like this:

https://open.spotify.com/playlist/4EW8ZodwjjPIEJQ2FZZH5H

Replace open.spotify.com with www.spotifyrecs.ml to get

https://www.spotifyrecs.ml/playlist/4EW8ZodwjjPIEJQ2FZZH5H

and voila! Start exploring!
